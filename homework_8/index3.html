EXPLORE THE ROMAN TIMBER TRUSS

Like all buildings which date back beyond the nineteenth century, before the use of industrialized materials like iron and steel as major structural components, great pieces of lumber were hewn to form magnificent structural elements.  Dates back to the Roman empire, people used those timber to build roof truss which is light and strong to bear the weight of the roof and can expand a large span across the main room of a church.
